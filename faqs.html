Got Questions?

Frequently
Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our process, products, and how to get started. Can't find the answer? Just get in touch.

What types of equipment do you offer?

We offer custom boxing and martial arts equipment, including gloves, pads, headgear, Gi's, protective gear, clothing, and more. Our range covers boxing, Muay Thai, BJJ, MMA, kickboxing, and all combat sports. If there's something specific you need that isn't listed, just get in touch — we can usually help.

Can I customise my equipment?

Absolutely — all of our products are fully customisable, including (but not limited to) the material, fit, weight, colour, branding, and more. We work with you to create something that perfectly represents your gym or team. Just send us your logo and we'll take it from there.

How do I place an order?

Simply fill in our enquiry form on the homepage with as much detail as possible — what you're looking for, quantities, and any branding ideas you have. We'll respond within 24 hours via email to discuss your requirements and get the process started. Alternatively, you can message us on WhatsApp at +44 7438 952003.

Can you visit my gym for a chat?

Yes! We're happy to visit gyms within central Scotland or within approximately 20 miles of Edinburgh. For customers further afield, we're equally happy to consult via phone, email, or video call. No matter where you are in the UK, we'll make the process smooth and straightforward.

Where are you based?

We're based in Edinburgh, Scotland — but we cover nationwide. Whether your gym is in London, Manchester, Cardiff, or anywhere else across the UK, we deliver directly to your door.

How does the process work?

Our process is simple and collaborative:

  1. Enquire — Send us your requirements via our contact form or WhatsApp.
  2. Discuss — We gather all the details: product type, quantity, materials, and your logo file.
  3. Design — Our designers create detailed mock-ups for your review. We refine until you're 100% happy.
  4. Sample — We offer a sample run before the full order so you can see and feel the quality firsthand.
  5. Produce & Deliver — Your finished product is manufactured and delivered directly to your gym.

Expedited orders are available if you need things on a tighter timeline — just let us know.

Are your products made with high quality materials?

Absolutely. All our products are crafted using top-grade materials to ensure durability, comfort, and performance. We work with experienced manufacturers and apply strict quality control at every stage of the process. We wouldn't put our name on anything we wouldn't be proud to use ourselves.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Yes — for sample orders, the minimum is 4 items (charged at a higher per-unit rate to cover setup costs). For main production orders, the minimum is 20 items. We also accommodate larger bulk orders. If you're unsure what quantity suits your needs, get in touch and we'll advise based on your budget and requirements.

How long does production and delivery take?

Lead times vary depending on the product type and order quantity, but we'll always give you a clear timeline before production begins. If you have a specific deadline — a gym opening, a tournament, or a team event — let us know upfront and we'll do everything we can to meet it, including expedited options where available.

Do I need to have a logo ready?

Ideally yes — a high-resolution version of your logo (vector format preferred) works best for embroidery and printing. However, if you don't have one yet, we can work with what you have or discuss options. The most important thing is getting the conversation started.

Still have questions? We'd love to hear from you.
