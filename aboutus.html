Our Story

Strength Meets
Craftsmanship

Crafted Athletic, proudly based in Edinburgh, is dedicated to supplying combat sports gyms and training centres across the UK with premium, custom-branded equipment built for durability and peak performance.

Our Mission

Built for Those Who Fight for It

Our mission is to craft durable, innovative, and personalised fighting equipment that supports the journey of athletes at every level — from the beginner stepping onto the mat for the first time, to the seasoned professional competing on the world stage.

We work closely with gym owners, coaches, and fighters to understand exactly what's needed — then we deliver on that promise with expert manufacturing and strict quality control at every stage of production.

Based in Edinburgh, we serve gyms and combat sports academies nationwide, offering flexible order sizes from small custom batches to full bulk production runs.

Our Vision

The Leading Name in Custom Combat Gear

We aspire to become the leading name in custom combat sports gear — recognised not just for our products, but for our commitment to quality, integrity, and innovation across everything we do.

Our goals are simple: expand our product range for the fighting community, invest in technologies and materials that enhance durability, establish lasting partnerships through consistent quality, and promote ethical sourcing throughout our supply chain.

  • Expanding product offerings for the fighting community
  • Investing in technologies and materials that enhance durability
  • Establishing lasting partnerships through quality and trust
  • Promoting ethical sourcing and sustainable production practices
What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Craftsmanship

Meticulous attention to detail in every product we produce. From the stitching on a boxing glove to the weave of a gi — quality is never an afterthought.

Performance

Equipment designed for serious athletes at every level. We don't compromise on materials or construction — your gear should work as hard as you do.

Trust

Honesty and accountability at every stage — from your first enquiry to final delivery. We say what we'll do, and we do what we say.

Adaptability

We listen to our clients and evolve with their needs. Whether it's a new product category or a specific customisation request — we find a way.

Sustainability

Responsible production practices and ethical sourcing are part of how we operate — building a business we're proud of for the long term.

Community

Combat sports gyms are more than businesses — they're communities. We're proud to serve them and to play a small part in the journeys they support.
