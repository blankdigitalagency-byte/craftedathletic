Custom training t-shirts from Crafted Athletic are the everyday workhorse of gym apparel. Lightweight, breathable, and built from moisture-wicking polyester blends, they keep athletes cool and comfortable through intense sessions — from pad work and bag training to strength and conditioning.

We offer both screen printing for bold, single or multi-colour designs and full sublimation for all-over patterns and gradient work. Either method is applied with precision and built to last — colours stay vivid through regular training and washing without cracking or peeling. Available in men's, women's, and unisex cuts with a full range of sizes, ensuring every member of your gym or team is covered. We work with you on collar style, sleeve length, and fit so the end result works as both training kit and casual wear.

The minimum order is 20 units, with samples available from 4. Whether you're an established gym looking to rebrand, a new academy building your identity, or a fight team heading to a competition — branded t-shirts are one of the most cost-effective ways to represent. We deliver nationwide across the UK from our Edinburgh base, and every order starts with a free design mock-up. Get in touch via our enquiry form or on WhatsApp to get started.
