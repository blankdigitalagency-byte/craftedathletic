Custom hoodies from Crafted Athletic do more than keep fighters warm on the way to training. They're a visible marker of gym identity — worn at competitions, on the high street, and everywhere in between. When your team steps into a venue wearing branded hoodies, it sends a message before the first bell rings.

We use heavyweight cotton-polyester blends that provide genuine warmth and durability — not the thin, disposable feel you get from fast-fashion alternatives. Available as pullovers, zip-ups, or sleeveless gilets, each style is cut for an athletic fit without being restrictive. Customisation covers the full garment — front and back prints, embroidered logos, sleeve branding, and hood detailing are all options.

The design process includes a detailed digital mock-up before anything goes to production, so you approve every element before a single stitch is made. Minimum order is 20 units with samples from 4. We work with boxing clubs, Muay Thai gyms, BJJ academies, and multi-discipline sports teams across the UK. Nationwide delivery from Edinburgh means straightforward logistics wherever you're based. If your gym is due a rebrand or you're building team identity from scratch, get in touch and we'll take it from there.
