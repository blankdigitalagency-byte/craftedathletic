Custom focus pads from Crafted Athletic are designed for coaches who demand the best from their training sessions and want their equipment to reflect the identity of their gym. Built around a high-density foam core, our pads absorb impact cleanly and consistently — whether you're working with a beginner or drilling combinations with a seasoned professional.

The ergonomic shape and adjustable wrist strap keep the pad securely positioned throughout drills, reducing fatigue and improving accuracy for both coach and fighter. The outer shell is crafted from durable leather or synthetic leather, resistant to the scuffs and wear that come with daily use.

Customisation is complete — your gym's logo, brand colours, and design details are integrated directly into the manufacturing process. Options include embroidery, screen printing, and full sublimation depending on your design. We supply boxing gyms, Muay Thai academies, kickboxing clubs, and multi-discipline training centres across the UK, with minimum orders from 20 units and samples available from 4. Nationwide delivery from Edinburgh means your order arrives promptly wherever you're based.
