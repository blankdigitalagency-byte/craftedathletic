A good headguard needs to do more than protect — it needs to stay in place, not restrict your vision, and hold up under repeated impact over months of training. Crafted Athletic's custom headguards are engineered with all of this in mind, using multi-layer foam padding across cheeks, temples, and chin for comprehensive coverage without the bulk.

Built for both sparring and competition use, our headguards offer adjustable sizing and a secure strap system that keeps the guard firmly positioned even during intense exchanges. The outer shell uses high-grade leather or synthetic leather, resistant to sweat, impact, and the daily wear of a busy training environment.

Every headguard is fully customisable — your gym's logo and colours are integrated cleanly and professionally, making them a visible statement of team identity on the gym floor and in competition. We offer open-face, closed-face, and cheek guard variations to suit your preference and sporting discipline. The minimum order is 20 units with samples from 4. We supply boxing gyms, Muay Thai gyms, and combat sports academies across the UK, delivering nationwide from Edinburgh.
