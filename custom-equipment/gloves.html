Custom Boxing Gloves

Premium leather boxing gloves built for the demands of intense training and competition. Every pair is fully customisable — material, weight, colour, padding, and branding are all tailored to your requirements.

Premium Materials
Top-grade leather or synthetic leather construction for maximum durability and feel.
Multi-layer Padding
Advanced foam layering provides superior knuckle protection and shock absorption.
Fully Custom Branded
Your gym's logo, colours, and design — embroidered or printed with precision.
Available Weights
8oz to 18oz — perfect for pad work, bag training, and sparring across all levels.

Custom boxing gloves from Crafted Athletic are built for gym owners, coaches, and fighters who refuse to compromise on quality or identity. Each pair starts with top-grade leather or premium synthetic leather — chosen for its durability, feel, and ability to hold colour and branding over thousands of rounds.

We offer gloves from 8oz through to 18oz, covering every training need from speed work and pad sessions to bag training and full sparring. Multi-layer foam padding protects knuckles without sacrificing wrist stability, and every glove is reinforced at the stress points where most cheaper products fail.

Your logo, colours, and design elements are applied with precision — embroidered or printed to a standard that holds up wash after wash, session after session. Whether you want a subtle gym badge or a bold, full-colour statement piece, we deliver. The minimum production order is 20 pairs, with samples available from 4 units so you can see and feel the quality before committing. We deliver nationwide across the UK from our Edinburgh base — gyms in London, Manchester, Glasgow, and everywhere in between count among our regular clients.
